What is PHP?
------------
- PHP stands for PHP: Hypertext Preprocessor
    - An open-source server-side scripting language
    - Used for developing web applications
    - It has been around since 1994


- PHP accounts for 70% of all websites including
    - Websites that use frameworks (e.g. Laravel)
    - Content management systems (e.g. Drupal & Wordpress)


- PHP can be used to build anything
    - From a simple contact form to a Social Network platform (e.g. Facebook)
